Help

Welcome to our Annotation Competition.

Our aim is to gamify the tagging of images. For this, we have two game modes. These are available after logging in.

Signup and Login

Choose a user name and a password. Requirements and helpful are shown on the signup pages. Both user name and password can later be changed. If you don't want to have an account anymore, this account can be deleted. The players with the highest scores are mentioned in the high score! Fun! You can change your account name and password at any time. You can also delete your account later.

Game modes

There are two game modes available: Classic and Reverse Captcha

Classic

An image is shown. The user may then provide tags describing the image. The app checks the tags and if they are accepted, you receive points based on a scoring function. A step-by-step guide can be found in the tutorial Scoring function: TODO

Reverse Captcha

To check the quality of the tags, you are shown a few images and the tags from one of the images. You have to match the tags to one of the images. This helps us in determining, which tags are helpful! Of course, you get points for this.

Settings
